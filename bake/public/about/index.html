Know the story behind Bake Natural

Hai Everyone,

I’m Yamini Durga, the creator of Bake Natural. I’m glad that you have taken time to know about Bake Natural I,m a food blogger. Blogging introduced me into the world of baking. Also I,m a mother of two kids. While baking at home for my kids, I wanted to cut down the negative things in baked goods as far as possible.

Baking cakes at home make it way more healthy because we do not add any chemicals like cake gel, shortening, preservatives, bread improver etc. But still the things made me feel guilty were all purpose flour (Maida) and white sugar.

We use whole wheat, millets and traditional rice varieties for our baked goods. We use raw khandsari sugar which is unprocessed and natural. Lessened the amount of sugar by including naturally sweet fruits like banana and jack fruit. These fruits not only reduce sugar but also give their good flavor and nutrition.
